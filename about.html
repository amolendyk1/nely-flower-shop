Our Story

The Bud & Bloom was born from Nely Molendyk's lifelong passion for flowers and her desire to share that joy with others. What started as a small garden of colorful blooms has blossomed into a full-fledged flower education and bouquet shop.

Nely believes that everyone has the ability to create something beautiful — they just need the right guidance, the right flowers, and a little bit of inspiration. Her mission is to make floral design accessible, educational, and fun for all.

Whether you're a seasoned florist, a weekend gardener, or someone looking for the perfect gift, The Bud & Bloom is here to help you connect with nature's most beautiful creations.

Our Mission

To educate, inspire, and empower everyone to explore the art of floristry — one bloom at a time.
