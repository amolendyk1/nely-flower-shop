Flowers

Your comprehensive guide to flowers — from growing conditions and care tips to perfect pairings for your next bouquet.

Rose

Rosa

Climate: Temperate, well-drained soil, full sun

Care: Prune regularly, water deeply once a week, protect from frost.

Pairs well with:

Lavender Baby's Breath Eucalyptus

Lavender

Lavandula

Climate: Mediterranean, dry soil, full sun

Care: Well-draining soil, prune after flowering, avoid over-watering.

Pairs well with:

Rose Sunflower Eucalyptus

Sunflower

Helianthus annuus

Climate: Warm, full sun, well-drained soil

Care: Water regularly, stake tall varieties, harvest seeds in autumn.

Pairs well with:

Lavender Daisy Baby's Breath

Tulip

Tulipa

Climate: Cool, well-drained soil, full to partial sun

Care: Plant bulbs in fall, deadhead after bloom, let foliage die back naturally.

Pairs well with:

Daffodil Hyacinth Iris

Daisy

Bellis perennis

Climate: Temperate, moist well-drained soil, full sun to partial shade

Care: Deadhead regularly, divide clumps every 2-3 years, water during dry spells.

Pairs well with:

Rose Lavender Sunflower

Peony

Paeonia

Climate: Cool temperate, fertile soil, full sun

Care: Plant in fall, support heavy blooms, cut back in autumn.

Pairs well with:

Rose Iris Baby's Breath

Hydrangea

Hydrangea macrophylla

Climate: Temperate, moist soil, morning sun/afternoon shade

Care: Keep soil consistently moist, prune after blooming, adjust soil pH for color.

Pairs well with:

Peony Rose Lavender

Lily

Lilium

Climate: Temperate, well-drained soil, full sun to partial shade

Care: Plant bulbs in deep soil, stake tall varieties, deadhead spent blooms, mulch to keep roots cool.

Pairs well with:

Rose Lavender Baby's Breath

Orchid

Orchidaceae

Climate: Tropical / humid, warm temperatures, indirect light

Care: Water sparingly — let roots dry between waterings. Use bark-based potting mix. Provide bright, indirect light.

Pairs well with:

Lily Hydrangea Rose

Carnation

Dianthus caryophyllus

Climate: Temperate, well-drained soil, full sun to partial shade

Care: Deadhead regularly, pinch back stems for bushier growth, water at base to avoid leaf spots.

Pairs well with:

Rose Baby's Breath Lavender

Cherry Blossom

Prunus serrulata

Climate: Temperate, cool winters, mild springs, well-drained soil

Care: Prune after flowering, water during dry spells, protect from strong winds. Blooms last 1-2 weeks.

Pairs well with:

Tulip Daffodil Iris

Hibiscus

Hibiscus rosa-sinensis

Climate: Tropical / subtropical, warm temperatures, high humidity, full sun

Care: Water frequently in warm months, prune in early spring, bring indoors in cold climates.

Pairs well with:

Orchid Lily Bird of Paradise

Calla Lily

Zantedeschia aethiopica

Climate: Warm temperate, moist soil, partial shade

Care: Keep soil consistently moist, fertilize monthly during growing season, cut back after flowering.

Pairs well with:

Rose Lily Hydrangea

Daffodil

Narcissus

Climate: Temperate, well-drained soil, full sun to partial shade

Care: Plant bulbs in fall, deadhead after bloom, let foliage die back naturally. Avoid cutting leaves until yellow.

Pairs well with:

Tulip Hyacinth Iris

Marigold

Tagetes

Climate: Warm, full sun, well-drained soil. Tolerates heat well.

Care: Deadhead spent blooms regularly, water at base, pinch back young plants for bushier growth.

Pairs well with:

Sunflower Daisy Lavender

Anthurium

Anthurium andraeanum

Climate: Tropical, high humidity, bright indirect light

Care: Keep soil evenly moist but not soggy, mist leaves regularly, fertilize monthly in growing season.

Pairs well with:

Orchid Lily Calla Lily

Iris

Iris germanica

Climate: Temperate, well-drained soil, full sun

Care: Plant rhizomes in late summer, divide every 3-4 years, avoid over-watering to prevent rot.

Pairs well with:

Peony Lily Daffodil

Lisianthus

Eustoma grandiflorum

Climate: Warm temperate, well-drained soil, full sun

Care: Water consistently, stake tall stems, deadhead spent blooms to encourage re-blooming.

Pairs well with:

Rose Eucalyptus Baby's Breath

Lotus

Nelumbo nucifera

Climate: Warm aquatic, full sun, still freshwater

Care: Grow in shallow ponds or large containers with aquatic soil. Fertilize monthly during bloom season. Protect from freezing temperatures.

Pairs well with:

Water Lily Calla Lily Iris
