Our Story

The Bud & Bloom was born from Nely Molendyk's lifelong passion for flowers and her desire to share that joy with others. What started as a small garden of colorful blooms has blossomed into a full-fledged flower education and bouquet shop.

Nely believes that everyone has the ability to create something beautiful — they just need the right guidance, the right flowers, and a little bit of inspiration.
