Flower Library

Learn about your favorite blooms — growing conditions, care instructions, and suggested pairings.

🌹

Rose

Rosa

Classic romantic bloom. Prefers full sun and well-drained soil. Pairs beautifully with lavender and eucalyptus.

💜

Lavender

Lavandula

Fragrant and calming. Thrives in full sun with minimal water. Complements roses and daisies in arrangements.

🌻

Sunflower

Helianthus

Cheerful and bold. Grows best in full sun. Pairs well with greenery and wildflowers for a rustic look.

🌸

Hydrangea

Hydrangea macrophylla

Lush, full blooms that love morning sun and afternoon shade. Perfect paired with peonies and roses.

🌷

Tulip

Tulipa

Elegant spring bloom. Prefers well-drained soil and full to partial sun. Complements daffodils and hyacinths.

🌼

Daisy

Bellis perennis

Simple and charming. Grows in almost any condition. Adds a cheerful touch to mixed bouquets.

🌺

Peony

Paeonia

Luxurious, fragrant blooms. Needs full sun and cold winters. Pairs beautifully with roses and hydrangeas.

🌿

Eucalyptus

Eucalyptus cinerea

Aromatic foliage favorite. Thrives in full sun. Used as a filler green in nearly every arrangement.

About Nely & The Bud & Bloom

The Bud & Bloom was born from Nely Molendyk's lifelong passion for flowers and her desire to share the art of floral design with others. What started as a small home garden has blossomed into a full-fledged flower education and bouquet shop.

Nely believes that everyone can learn to arrange beautiful bouquets. Her hands-on classes teach the fundamentals of color theory, flower compatibility, and care techniques — giving students the confidence to create their own stunning arrangements.

Whether you're shopping for a handcrafted bouquet, looking to learn a new skill, or simply want to brighten your home with plants, The Bud & Bloom is here to help you grow.
