Plants

Your guide to selecting and caring for beautiful plants — from indoor greenery to outdoor garden favorites.

💜

Lavender

Lavandula angustifolia

Climate: Mediterranean / warm temperate. Thrives in hot, dry summers.

Sun: Full sun (6+ hours daily)

Water: Low — water deeply but infrequently. Let soil dry between waterings.

Soil: Well-draining, slightly alkaline. Sandy or gravelly soil preferred.

Care tips: Prune after flowering to maintain shape. Mulch with gravel, not bark. Protect from winter wetness.

🌵

Succulent

Various species

Climate: Arid / dry indoor. Perfect for beginners and low-light homes.

Sun: Bright, indirect light to direct sun (varies by species)

Water: Very low — water every 2-3 weeks. Allow soil to completely dry.

Soil: Cactus / succulent mix with sand and perlite for drainage.

Care tips: Overwatering is the #1 killer. Use pots with drainage holes. Reduce watering in winter.

🌿

Fern

Nephrolepis exaltata

Climate: Tropical / humid. Loves moisture and warmth.

Sun: Indirect light or partial shade. No direct sun.

Water: High — keep soil consistently moist. Mist leaves regularly.

Soil: Rich, well-draining potting mix with organic matter.

Care tips: Brown tips mean dry air — increase humidity. Group with other plants to raise moisture. Feed monthly in spring/summer.

🌹

Rose Bush

Rosa

Climate: Temperate. Needs a cold dormant period for best blooms.

Sun: Full sun (6+ hours)

Water: Moderate — deep watering at the base, 1-2 times per week.

Soil: Rich, loamy, well-draining soil with compost.

Care tips: Prune in early spring. Remove spent blooms to encourage re-blooming. Watch for aphids and black spot.

🐍

Snake Plant

Sansevieria trifasciata

Climate: Arid / tropical. Extremely adaptable indoors.

Sun: Low to bright indirect light — very forgiving.

Water: Very low — water every 3-4 weeks. Let soil dry completely.

Soil: Well-draining cactus mix or general potting soil with sand.

Care tips: Nearly indestructible. Excellent air purifier. Keep away from pets (mildly toxic). Reduce watering in winter.

💙

Hydrangea Shrub

Hydrangea macrophylla

Climate: Temperate, cool summers. Prefers mild coastal climates.

Sun: Morning sun, afternoon shade

Water: High — keep soil moist. Wilting leaves signal thirst.

Soil: Rich, moist, well-draining. Acidic soil produces blue blooms; alkaline soil produces pink.

Care tips: Prune after blooming. Add aluminum sulfate for blue flowers, lime for pink. Protect from harsh afternoon sun.
