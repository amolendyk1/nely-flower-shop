Plants

Your guide to selecting and caring for beautiful plants — from indoor greenery to outdoor garden favorites.

Snake Plant

Sansevieria trifasciata

Climate: Arid / tropical. Extremely adaptable indoors.

Sun: Low to bright indirect light — very forgiving.

Water: Very low — water every 3-4 weeks. Let soil dry completely.

Soil: Well-draining cactus mix or general potting soil with sand.

Care tips: Nearly indestructible. Excellent air purifier. Keep away from pets (mildly toxic). Reduce watering in winter.

Striped Calathea

Goeppertia picturata

Climate: Tropical / humid. Thrives in warm, moisture-rich environments.

Sun: Low to bright indirect light. No direct sun.

Water: Moderate — keep soil evenly moist. Use distilled or filtered water to prevent leaf tip burn.

Soil: Peat-based potting mix with perlite for drainage. Slightly acidic preferred.

Care tips: High humidity is essential — mist daily or use a pebble tray. Wipe leaves gently to keep them shiny. Avoid cold drafts.

Anthurium

Anthurium andraeanum

Climate: Tropical / humid. Thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light.

Sun: Bright indirect light. No direct sun.

Water: Moderate — keep soil evenly moist. Mist regularly to maintain humidity.

Soil: Well-draining, peat-based potting mix with perlite.

Care tips: Wipe leaves with a damp cloth to keep pores clean. Fertilize monthly in spring and summer. Repot every 2 years.
